# DeepDive — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Solo crypto trader (single-user app). Uses this during active trading sessions — scanning signals, checking portfolio composition, tracking paper trade performance. Context: low-light environment, likely at a desk or on mobile. Decision-making under uncertainty is the job. The interface must not add to the cognitive load.

### Brand Personality
Not "modern, elegant, calm" as adjectives — those are descriptions of outcomes. The underlying personality is: **dispassionate, exacting, unhurried**. Like a precision instrument. A high-end oscilloscope, a finely calibrated chromatography readout, a Bloomberg terminal for someone who prefers silence. It doesn't celebrate gains. It doesn't panic at losses. It just shows you what is.

**Anti-reference**: Coinbase — friendly, colorful, consumer-facing. That product wants to make crypto approachable. This product assumes you already know what you're doing.

**References**: Binance, TradingView, KuCoin — dark, data-dense, clean hierarchy, monospaced numbers, accent color used only for active state.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Dark theme** — mandatory. This is a trading tool used during active sessions.
- **Flat structure** — no card containers. Rows, tables, hairline dividers. Content is the container.
- **Color philosophy**: accent color (steel blue, hue 210) appears only for active/highlighted state. Everything else is in the neutral palette.
- **No decoration for its own sake** — no glassmorphism, no gradient text, no rounded drop shadows.

### Design Principles
1. **Data first** — Every visual element serves the data. If it doesn't help interpret a number or a relationship, it doesn't belong.
2. **Typography does the heavy lifting** — Hierarchy through weight, size, and tracking. Not color, not background fills, not borders.
3. **Mono for numbers** — All financial figures in JetBrains Mono, no exceptions. Alignment is legibility.
4. **Flat structure** — No nested cards. Rows and hairline dividers. The list IS the container.
5. **Precision over decoration** — Labels are 11px, uppercase, letter-spaced. Numbers are large, exact, tabular.
